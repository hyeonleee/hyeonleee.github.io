About

He is currently working at Korea Ministry of National Defense, Seoul, Korea. Before the current work, he has three years of research and development experience with Agency for Defense Development (ADD) as a researcher. Since 2017, he has been a Ph.D student in the School of Cybersecurity, Korea University, Seoul, Korea. His current research interests include network security, game theory, and cyber defense.
